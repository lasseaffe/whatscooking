2026-04-15 - Logic: Pantry-First Search (Scramble)
Achievement: Implemented Pantry-First Search algorithm in GET /api/pantry/scramble. Scores recipes by ingredient overlap percentage using ingredient-substitutes.ts substitution map. Substitution scoring accounts for ingredient categories (e.g., any milk → oat milk). Returns ranked recipe suggestions from pantry contents.

Achievement: Built cost-estimator.ts with category-based pricing engine. Assigns per-unit costs to ingredients by category (produce, protein, dairy, pantry). Powers GET /api/budget endpoint. Plugs into recipe cards for per-serving cost display.
