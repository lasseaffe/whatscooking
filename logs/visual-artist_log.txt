BRAND STANDARDS:
  Photography: Warm, editorial, overhead or 45-degree angle. Natural lighting. Artisan/rustic props.
  Colors: Walnut & Linen palette only. No saturated primaries.
  Icons: Lucide (consistent stroke weight). 16px card use, 20px panels, 24px nav.
  Gradients: Linear bottom-to-top, rgba(0,0,0,0.7) for text overlay.
  Logo: Oval badge format with cook figure — rounded, artisan aesthetic.
