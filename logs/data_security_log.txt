2026-04-21 - Audit: Initial PII Inventory
Achievement: Completed first-pass PII mapping across all Supabase tables.

  PERSONAL DATA FIELDS IDENTIFIED:
  auth.users (Supabase managed): email, created_at, last_sign_in — managed by Supabase, subject to their DPA
  pantry_items: user_id (pseudonym), name (ingredient names — low risk), expiry_date (non-personal)
  calorie_entries: user_id + calories + meal_type + logged_at
    → SPECIAL CATEGORY RISK: dietary/health data under Art. 9 GDPR — requires explicit consent or medical necessity basis
  weight_logs: user_id + weight_kg + logged_at
    → SPECIAL CATEGORY RISK: health data — explicit consent required (Art. 9(2)(a))
  dinner_parties: title, date, location (text) — location could be home address → MEDIUM risk
  party_guests: user_id + rsvp_status — low risk but reveals social relationships
  recipes (user-created): user_id + source_url — source_url may reveal social media identity

2026-04-21 - Audit: Right to Erasure (Art. 17 DSGVO)
Achievement: Reviewed account deletion flow.

  FINDING [CRITICAL]: No DELETE /api/profile or account deletion endpoint found.
  RECOMMENDATION: Implement account deletion flow that:
    1. Deletes all rows in: pantry_items, calorie_entries, weight_logs, meal_plans, meal_entries, saves, ratings, comments, swipe_history, party_guests (where user_id = requesting user)
    2. Anonymizes user-created recipes: set user_id = NULL, keep recipe content (legitimate archival interest)
    3. Calls Supabase Admin API to delete auth.users record
    4. Completes within 30 days of request (Art. 17 deadline)
    5. Sends confirmation email to user

  FINDING [HIGH]: CASCADE DELETE not confirmed on all tables.
  RECOMMENDATION: Data Steward to verify FK cascade rules on all user_id foreign keys.
