Product Feature Overview · 2026

What's Cooking
The Intelligent Kitchen OS

Every feature follows the natural flow of a meal — from discovering what to cook, through cooking it, to clearing up after. No hunting for tools. Each phase is where you'd expect it.

I
Discover
Find what to cook tonight
🔥
Hot Right Now
A horizontally scrolling strip of the highest-ranked recipes on the platform, updated in real time by community ratings. Appears at the top of Discover so the best food is always front and centre.
Live ranking · ranked_score
🥬
Pantry-First Search
Toggle "Pantry-First" in Discover to filter the recipe grid to only dishes you can already mostly make. Every recipe card shows a pantry match percentage (green 80%+, amber 50–79%) so you see at a glance how stocked you are.
Requires pantry items · zero waste
⭐
Premium Instagram Collection
800+ hand-picked recipes sourced from elite food creators. Each card links to the original post. Use the AI Extract button to reconstruct full ingredients and step-by-step instructions from any post caption.
AI extraction · Gemini
⚡
Kitchen Hacks
A curated library of viral techniques (like the salt-crust method or dry-brine trick) displayed as quick-expand cards. Extract any hack into step-by-step instructions directly in the app.
Expandable · in-page modal
🌍
World Cuisines
Filter the entire recipe library by cuisine — from Moroccan tagines to Japanese ramen. Each cuisine card shows a hero image, signature dishes, and a one-line character description.
25+ cuisines · regional grouping
🌿
Seasonal Filter
Highlights recipes that use ingredients currently in season in your region. Reduces shopping cost and supports local produce — what's good right now is always visible.
Produce-aware · regional
II
Prepare
Get your kitchen ready
🧺
Pantry Manager
Track everything you own — categorised by type (produce, dairy, dry goods etc.) with category icons and colours. What's in your pantry drives the Pantry-First filter in Discover, so your inventory directly shapes what you can cook.
Category icons · quantity tracking
🥗
Adapted Ingredients
On each recipe page, your active dietary mode (vegan, gluten-free, etc.) is applied to the ingredient list with visual callouts — so you know before starting whether a swap is needed.
Dietary-aware · emoji icons
⏱
Time Breakdown Bar
A visual bar splitting total time into prep and cook segments, with estimated hands-on effort. Instantly shows whether a recipe is quick-fire or a slow weekend project.
Prep + cook split
📅
Meal Planner
Build weekly meal plans with AI-suggested recipes. The AI avoids repeating the same recipe across days, prioritises ★ MEAL-PREP flagged dishes for lunches, and varies cuisines and protein sources automatically.
AI-suggested · no repeats
III
Cook
Step-by-step execution
🚇
Subway Roadmap
Recipe instructions rendered as an interactive subway map. Click or tap any station to jump to that step. Completed steps show a checkmark and strike-through. A progress bar shows overall cooking progress. Inline cooking-technique tips appear for the active step.
Sage & Walnut palette · prev/next nav
📖
Living Cookbook Ticker
A rotating carousel of community cooking wisdom — tips like "let meat rest before cutting" or "bloom your spices first" that appear above the instructions. Rotates every 8 seconds with a slide-fade transition and dot navigation.
10 tips · 8s interval · manual nav
💡
Inline Technique Tips
As you progress through the Subway Roadmap, cooking techniques detected in each step (e.g. "sauté", "deglaze", "fold") trigger contextual pop-up tips explaining the technique — so you learn while you cook, not before. Each tip appears only once per recipe to avoid repetition.
Auto-detected · deduplicated
IV
Serve
Plate it. Rate it. Share it.
🕯️
Table Stylist
Three occasion modes — Casual, Intimate, Festive — each with tailored plating advice: which plate to use, how to garnish, layout guidance, drink pairings, and a pro plating tip. The app auto-detects the occasion from the recipe title so the right mode is pre-selected.
Casual · Intimate · Festive
⭐
Recipe Ratings
Rate any recipe across four dimensions: taste, difficulty, value for effort, and presentation. Aggregate scores are displayed on the recipe page and feed directly into the Hot Right Now ranking algorithm.
4-axis · feeds ranking
💬
Community Comments
Leave tips, tweaks, and variations on any recipe. Comments show the author's name and are visible to everyone — forming the feedback loop that makes recipes get better over time.
Real-time · attributed
🔖
Recipe Saves
Bookmark any recipe to your saved collection with one tap. Saved recipes are synced across devices and accessible from your profile — a personal cookbook that builds itself as you explore.
Instant · synced
V
Restore
Store leftovers. Reset the kitchen.
❄️
Zero-Waste Storage Guide
Auto-detects the recipe category (meat, fish, pasta, rice, soup, salad, baked, curry) and displays: how many days it will keep in the fridge, the best reheating method, and a zero-waste tip specific to that food type.
8 categories · AI-calculated
🧹
Cleanup Concierge
Toggle between Dishwasher and Hand-Wash modes. Each mode shows a Safe list and a No-Go list of common kitchenware — so you never ruin a cast iron pan or dull a good knife again. No separate research needed.
Dishwasher · Hand-wash modes
✅
Kitchen Reset Button
A single button to mark your kitchen as fully reset. Triggers a satisfying confirmation state with a celebration icon — a small ritual that closes the loop on the cooking session and builds positive habit reinforcement.
Habit loop · celebration state
